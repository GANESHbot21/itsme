W3schools

W3Schools is a popular resource for learning web development because it offers tutorials that are clear and easy to follow. It covers a wide range of topics including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, and newer frameworks and libraries. The interactive examples and simple explanations make it accessible for beginners and those wanting to refresh their knowledge


GeekforGeeks

GeeksforGeeks is another excellent resource, especially because of its wide variety of examples, code snippets, and in-depth explanations. It’s especially popular for learning data structures, algorithms, and computer science fundamentals. GeeksforGeeks also covers web development topics like JavaScript, React, and more, making it a versatile platform for both beginner and advanced learners.



Chat GPT

ChatGPT can be a powerful tool for finding solutions to a wide range of problems, from coding and web development issues to general knowledge and everyday questions. Its vast knowledge base helps it provide explanations, troubleshoot errors, and even suggest creative approaches to problems.
